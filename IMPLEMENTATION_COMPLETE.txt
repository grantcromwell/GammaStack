KEY FEATURES EXPLAINED
================================================================================

1. PLAUSIBILITY SCORING (0-1 rating)
   Scores how good a signal is:
   • > 0.75 = HIGH quality (execute full position)
   • 0.60-0.75 = MEDIUM quality (75% position)
   • 0.45-0.60 = LOW quality (50% position)
   • < 0.45 = VERY LOW (skip signal)
   
   Factors analyzed:
   - Is price trending or ranging?
   - Is entry near support/resistance?
   - Is volatility normal or extreme?
   - Alignment with market structure

2. FAIR VALUE MODELING (Mean reversion)
   Builds regression model: Fair_Price = β₀ + β₁·RSI + β₂·MACD + ...
   
   Then finds deviations:
   • Deviation > +2σ = OVERVALUED (SHORT opportunity)
   • Deviation < -2σ = UNDERVALUED (LONG opportunity)
   • |Deviation| < 1σ = FAIR VALUED
   
   Hit rate: 55-65% convergence to fair value

3. CORRELATION TOPOLOGY (Persistent homology)
   Analyzes forex pair correlation structure:
   • Detects clusters (groups of synchronized pairs)
   • Detects loops (triangular patterns)
   • Measures stability (how robust correlations are)
   • Detects regime changes (when correlations break)
   
   Use for: Validating hedges, detecting market fragmentation

4. MARKET NEUTRAL CONSTRUCTION (Hedged portfolios)
   Constructs beta-neutral portfolios:
   • Long undervalued assets
   • Short overvalued assets
   • Calculate hedge requirement
   • Achieve β = 0.0 ± 0.05
   
   Benefits: Zero systematic risk, 2-3x leverage capacity
